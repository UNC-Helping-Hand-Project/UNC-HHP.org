Help raise awareness for the upper limb difference community and give these children the opportunity to fully embrace their authentic self. You can financially support our organization through our nonprofit (Make sure to specify the UNC Chapter sent you!) and/or emailing helpinghandprojectunc@gmail.com about other giving options such as materials , filament, or equipment for 3D printing. Any contribution helps our cause and is tax deductible.
